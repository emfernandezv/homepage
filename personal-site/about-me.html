01. Who am I?

Howdy! My name is Eduardo and I enjoy solving problems and improving processes. I discovered my interest in software development in 2009 when I started customizing a local server (hosted in my desktop) for a popular MMOPRG game. For some reason, I was able to understand part of the C# code it was coded.

In 2010, I started my professional formation in Software Engineering and in 2011 started my professional path on different positions in leading companies of different industries that had in-house software developed in PowerBuilder which I had mastered during my first month as a Junior Developer.

Since then, I have been working on different projects for different industry companies such as Human Resources, Multi-industries corporations, Energy, Industrial Production, Manufacturing, and Religious Cults.

I am currently working as an Interface Delivery Analyst Sr. at a leading Health Industry Software Company sharing my experience and helping other developers to interface their custom apps with us. During this time, I have been thinking that even though my career had had a big push into the labor field, my tools weren't enough to keep up, that's why I decided to continue my professional formation to learn newer technologies to help more companies around the world.
